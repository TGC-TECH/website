Intro to 3D printing

In this 6-7 hour course students will learn about the technology behind 3D printers, This course can be broken up into 2 sessions. Students will learn about RAMPS 1.4, Marlin software, and Stepper motors by building their own 3D printer kit, they will then learn CAD in Autodesk 123D, they will learn about slicer options and how they affect print quality and strength using CURA.
