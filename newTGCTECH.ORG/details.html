More About Me!

Hello there! I am Toby Cowles, a high school senior and this is a site that I made. I am an electrical hobbyist who enjoys building with Arduino, Raspberry Pi and 3D printers. Some of the recent projects I have completed include a smart network rebooter, a 3x3 LED cube, a 3D printed laser engraver, and a smart mirror. I often publish the code for my projects on my Github and any CAD files on my Thingiverse. I also have experience teaching coding and electronics classes at Darien Library, Zaniac Greenwich and Rippowam Labs Makerspace. I have at least some experience in many popular programming languages but am most comfortable in the Arduino IDE. Additionally, I have dabbled in PCB design with KiCAD and have designed 3D models in 123D and Fusion360. I also enjoy being an amature sys-admin for my home network, which is centered around a Proxmox server that hosts LXC containers and VMs that gets used as a NAS, DNS server, Minecraft server, print server, RTMP server, and a remote desktop. And I have fairly extensive experience with computer building, upgrading and repair. If you would like to contact me, you can do so via email (toby@cowles.io)
