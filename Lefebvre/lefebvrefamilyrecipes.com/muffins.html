Muffins

2 cups flour
4 teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon salt
½ cup sugar
1 cup milk
1 egg
¼ cup melted butter
Sift dry ingredients combine milk and well beaten egg, blend in butter.
Add to dry ingredients and beat enough to mix well spoon into well greased muffin tins, filling 2/3 full
Bake for 35mins at 375
Yield 12 muffins
