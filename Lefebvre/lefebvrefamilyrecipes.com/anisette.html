Anisette Cookies

Heat oven to 350
A dozen of eggs
2 cups of sugar
2 sticks of butter
1 cup of oil
14 teaspoons of baking powder
8 cups of sifted flour
Salt
1 teaspoon of anisette
1 teaspoon of vanilla

Tip: Beat eggs sugar and butter and oil together sift flour, salt and baking powder combine all ingredients
