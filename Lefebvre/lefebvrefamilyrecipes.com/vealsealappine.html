Veal Sealappine Alla Marsala

1 pound veal for 3 servings cut into thin slices
Flour for dredging salt and freshly ground pepper
3 tablespoons butter
¼ cup marsalla wine
2 table canned concentrated boallon
1. Pound veal lightly until very tin dredge with flour seasoned with salt and pepper
2. heat butter in skillet add veal and brown on both sides add mnarsalla and cook on minute longer over medium high heat transfer meat to warm platter
3. Add boulion to pan dripping scrape loose all the brown particles and bring to boil pour over veal
4. take mushrooms sauté ½ pound slices mushrooms in 2 tables butter arrange on cooked veal
20 mins at 350
