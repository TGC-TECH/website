Pasta Chichita
(Otherwise know as: Pass the Cheese, or Hot Water)

Boil water Once water is boiled then put one box of Ditalini Barilla pasta in
While that is cooking cut up 2 cloves of garlic into small pieces
Then heat a frying pan with olive oil
Once the olive oil is hot put in the garlic and let it cook. Do not burn the garlic
Once the pasta is done, drain a little bit of the water
Then put the olive oil and the garlic into the pot with the water
Add 1 can of Progresso White kidney Cannellini beans to the pot
Then put some hot pepper, regular pepper, and salt
Then let every thing cook for a few minutes and then serve
