Pesto

2 cups packed fresh basil leaves
2 large garlic cloves
½ cup pine nuts
¾ cup grated parmesan cheese
2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil

Tip: Combine basil and garlic in the work bowl of a processor and blend until a paste. Scrape down sides. Add pine nuts and parmesan and process until smooth. With machine running add oil in a fine stream through the feed tube. Mix until smooth.
