Homemade Bread

5 pounds of flour
4 cups water warm not boiled
Pinch of salt 4 or 5 eggs makes bread soft
3 eggs or 4 to 5 for easter
½ sticks of margarine soft
Cut yest to bend in top of finger
Then cut in ½ melt in water
In December used ¾ to all of the yeast use all
Make well and put in all ingredients
Make cross put flour in pan
Let use to then kneed put in pans butter pans first let rise
375-400 degrees
Bake at 400 for 40-45 mins
Raise rack in lower oven do not over cook makes it hard
Cover bread with towels after removed from oven helps to keep it soft.

Tips: Watch dough bounces back means risen white sheet and blanket after 2 hours needs again let rise and need again let rise and roll to place in time bake
