Cream Puffs

Bake at 400
1 cup water
1 cup flour
½ cup butter
4 eggs medium
Boil butter of water
Stir in flour to make ball
Remove from heat
Add 1 egg at a time beating each egg until well blended
Tablespoon onto un-greased cookie sheet 40-45 mins

Italian Cream Filling
2 cups milk 1/3 cup flour dash salt
1 cup sugar
2 eggs beaten
Teaspoon vanilla
Mix flour, sugar and salt together
Scald milk and slowly stir in dry ingredients stir in eggs and vanilla and cook over low heat until thickened stirring constantly.
Allow time to cool
