Butter Ball

2 cups flour
2 teaspoon vanilla
2 margarine or butter sticks soft
½ cup chopped walnuts
¼ cup confectionery sugar
¼ cup sugar cut in batter, vanilla, sugar, add flour gradually, mix good, add nuts, do not grease pan
Set oven to 325
Cook for 25 mins just until light brown on bottom sit for 5 mins
Roll in confectionery sugar place neatly makes 5 doz
