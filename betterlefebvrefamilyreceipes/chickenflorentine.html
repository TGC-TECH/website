Chicken Florentine

Makes 4 servings

½ cup chopped onion 1 clove garlic, minced
1tbs butter
2 tbs cornstarch
1 13 ¾ oz. can chicken broth
¼ tsp dried basil leaves
½ cup shredded mozzarella
1 ½ cup cooked chicken strips
4 oz. chopped fresh spinach
1 cup tomatoes
Hot cooked pasta

In a large skillet over medium heat cook onion and garlic in margarine until tender about 5 mins. Stir in cornstarch mix in broth and basil. Cook and stir until mixture boils and thickens, stir in cheese until melted. Add chicken spinach and tomatoes cover and cook 1 min or until heated through. Serve over pasta .
