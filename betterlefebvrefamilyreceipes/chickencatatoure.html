Chicken Catatoure

½ cup olive oil
½ cup butter
2 cups finely chopped onion
2 green peppers chopped
4 garlic cloves mashed
½ teaspoon basil
1 cup canned tomatoes
¼ cup dry red wine
1 teaspoon salt and freshly ground black pepper

Sauté chicken in combined olive oil and butter for 10 mins. Or until golden brown. Add onions, green pepper, garlic, basil, salt and pepper and simmer for 5 mins. Add tomatoes bring to boil reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 mins. Stirring occasionally add wine and simmer for 10 mins. .
