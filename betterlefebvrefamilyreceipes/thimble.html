Thimble Cookies

Makes 2 dozen
Cook at 375
½ cup of sugar
2 sticks of butter or margarine
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 egg yokes
2 cups flour
Beat all this well

Dip balls in egg whites then in walnuts meats and bake for 5 mins then put hole in each and return to oven for 10 mins .
