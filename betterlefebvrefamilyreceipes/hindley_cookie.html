HINDLEY COOKIE

1 C. butter or margarine
1 tsp. salt
1 C. sugar
2 C. flour
1 C. brown sugar, packed
1 C. quick oats
2 eggs
1 C. Rice Krispies
1 ½ tsp. vanilla
1 C. chocolate chips
1 ¼ tsp. baking soda
1 C. M & M’s


Cream together butter and sugars. Add eggs one at a time with vanilla. Add all dry ingredients. By hand, stir in oats, Krispies, chips and M&M’s. Place approximately ¼ cup of dough on cookie sheet for each cookie. Flatten each ball slightly and bake at 350 degrees for 12 – 14 minutes. Let cool slightly before removing from cookie sheet. Once cooled, wrap each cookie individually with clear plastic wrap. Each batch should make around 20 cookies.
Hints: Use a heaping ice cream scoop to scoop the right amount of dough. After cooled, cookies can be placed in individual baggies then frozen (a dozen together) in large freezer bags. Take out of freezer to defrost the night before the sale. .
