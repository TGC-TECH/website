Pineapple Banana Muffins

Pineapple Banana Muffins
½ cup sugar
1 egg
½ cup oil
1 teasppon baking powder
1 scant teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
1 ½ cup flour
1 ripe banana mashed
1 cup un-drained crushed pineapple
Mix altogether
Bake for 25 mins or until done at 375
It makes 12 muffins .
