Clam Sauce

½ pound spaghetti
2 cans 7 ½ oz. Doxie minced
¼ cup olive oil clams
¼ cup butter
2 cloves garlic
1 tablespoons parsley
1 ½ teaspoon salt

Drain clams but keep ¼ cup of liquid, heat oil and butter add garlic until brown stir in liquid from clams and parsley and salt bring to boil in pot after boil turn to simmer uncovered for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes add clams simmer for 3 minutes.
