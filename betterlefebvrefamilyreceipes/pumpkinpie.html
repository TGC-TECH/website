Pumpkin Pie

1 can pumpkin
1 cup of brown granulated sugar
½ teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon ginger
2 table grammas mollios
In separate bowl
Beat 3 eggs
Put into mixture with 1 cup evaporated milk
Pour in pie 425 for 1 hour
.
