Tomato Sauce

1 pound of short ribs trim fat if needed
Brown ribs with a piece of garlic in olive oil
Once browned pour 3 can of Contadina crushed tomato’s in pot
1 can of sclafani plum tomato. Crush with your hands
Put a little bit of basial
Pinch of oregano, salt, red pepper
Make sure you stir but bring sauce to boil on low to medium heat
Then put about 1 tablespoon of backing powder into the sauce to take out the acid
If you are making meat balls one the meat balls are done put them in after all the acid is out and have them cook on low heat for about 20m mins .
