Spare Ribs

1 lb. meat per person
2 stripes cut
1 lemon squeezed
1 large onion chopped
Brown at 450 for ½ hour

Sauce
Salt and pepper
1 teaspoon chilly powder
1 teaspoon celery seed
¼ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white vinegar
¼ cup mor. Sauce
1 cup ketchup
1 cup water
Couple drops of Tabasco
350 for 1 ½ hours .
