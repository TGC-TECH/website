Mom's Chicken Soup

Place 4 breast of chicken (bone in) in large pot of water
Add 3 large onions (quartered length wise)
Bring to boil on medium/high
Skim off foam and discard.
Add 4-6 buillion cubes a few at a time as needed by scooping water from the pot into a measuring cup to dissolve cubes.
Pour into pot and stir.
Add salt, pepper, and fresh parsley.
Cook for ½ hour (approx.) on med./high heat.
Add carrots (almost entire bag of peeled baby carrots)
Add celery cut off tips use tops and leaves. - Do not use bottom halves.
Cook on medium heat for 'awhile'.
Turn down to low heat when vegetables seem cooked.
Cook noodles on acini Pepe or egg noodles
Drain pasta
To Serve soup add pasta cut chicken one piece at a time and add in to each dish. Enjoy!
