Artichoke Pie

Orongue deep dish pie
1 can artichoke hearts-pope
2 cloves garlic
6 eggs
8 oz. whole milk mozzarella
½ cup parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper

Sauté garlic and artichoke hearts in oil until soft and mash together beat eggs and add rest of ingredients put top on bake for a ½ hour at 375. Wait 5-10 minutes before cutting. .
