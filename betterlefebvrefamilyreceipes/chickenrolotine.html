Chicken Rolotine

Boneless cut chicken breasts
In ½ line on wax paper put Swiss cheese and boiled ham or prosuitto and mozzarella roll and put toothpicks then dredge in flour melt 1 stick of butter for 8 or 10 breasts sauté breasts in butter until brown and put in casserole.
10 breasts take two chicken boullion cubs and 2 cups of hot water melt cubs.
Put cubs in drippings and crape pan strain over rolletine bake in oven at 350 for ½ hour .
