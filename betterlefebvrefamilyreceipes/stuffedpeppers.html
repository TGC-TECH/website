Stuffed Peppers

1 pound of meat loaf
Salt and pepper
3 eggs
Hand full of cheese
Hand full of bread crumbs
Heat oven at 375
Put oil in pan and oil salt into pepper
Use red pepper to stuff
Cook for 45 mins to an hour

You can put gravy on then cook 15mins before you take out of oven. .
