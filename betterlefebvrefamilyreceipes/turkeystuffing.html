Turkey Stuffing

3 pounds sausage
1 big yellow Bermuda Onion chop small
Unseasoned cubed Pepperidge Farm Stuffing
Butter
Olive Oil
Chicken broth
Celery
Carrots
Vermouth
Sherry or Port
3-4 eggs

Take sausage put of casing and brown. Remove from pan. Drain oil add olive oil and butter in onions in pan brown a little and put in Vermouth ¼ cup. 3 big stalk of celery cut and 3 carrots cut into ¼ thick sauté until soft add ¼ cup sherry or port remove from pan and put all ingredients into bowl add ½ Pepperidge Farm stuffing. Beat 3-4 eggs and moisten with chicken stock a little at a time. .
